# Security Policy

## Supported versions
Only the latest main branch is supported with security updates.

## Reporting a vulnerability
- Email the maintainers at rainwears@support.test with a clear description, reproduction steps, and potential impact.
- Please do not open a public issue for vulnerabilities until a fix is released.
- You will receive an acknowledgement within 5 business days.

## Expectations
- We will investigate and prioritize fixes based on severity and impact.
- We may request additional details or a proof of concept to reproduce the issue.
